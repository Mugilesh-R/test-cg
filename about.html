About Serevia Export & Import

A horticulture-led export company delivering export-grade coconuts and fresh produce to global markets.

Who We Are

Serevia Export & Import is a professionally managed export company specialising in coconuts and fresh agricultural produce from India. Led by horticulture graduates, we bring strong technical knowledge of crop production, post-harvest handling, grading, and export suitability into every shipment we supply.

Our academic and practical background in horticulture allows us to understand crops beyond sourcing — from varietal selection and maturity standards to shelf life, quality retention, and destination-specific requirements. This knowledge-driven approach ensures consistency, reliability, and export-grade quality for our buyers.

We work directly with farmers and handling partners to maintain strict control over sourcing, processing, and dispatch, enabling us to supply products that meet international buyer expectations.

Our Mission

Our mission is to supply premium-quality coconuts and fresh produce to international markets by combining agricultural expertise, strict quality control, and customer-focused service.

  • Maintain uncompromised quality at every stage
  • Ensure reliable and timely deliveries
  • Meet buyer-specific specifications and standards
  • Build long-term, trust-based partnerships

Customer satisfaction remains central to our sourcing, handling, packaging, and export decisions.
