About Serevia Export & Import

Driven by agricultural expertise. Trusted by global buyers.

Who We Are

Serevia Export & Import is a horticulture-driven export company specializing in export-grade coconuts and fresh agricultural produce. Our operations are led by professionals with formal academic training in crop production, post-harvest handling, and quality management.

This technical foundation allows us to go beyond basic sourcing. We focus on varietal selection, maturity standards, shelf life, and destination-market suitability to deliver consistent, export-quality products.

Our Mission

Our mission is to supply premium-quality coconuts and fresh produce to international markets through strict quality control, dependable logistics, and customer-focused service.

  • ✔ Maintain uncompromised export quality
  • ✔ Ensure reliable and timely shipments
  • ✔ Meet buyer-specific requirements
  • ✔ Build long-term global partnerships

Serevia Export & Import is a trusted supplier of export-grade coconuts and fresh agricultural produce. We ensure consistent quality, reliable volumes, and international compliance for global markets.
