About Serevia Export & Import

Driven by agricultural expertise. Trusted by global buyers.

Who We Are

We are a horticulture-graduate-led export company specialising in coconuts and fresh agricultural produce. With formal academic training in crop production, post-harvest handling, and food quality management, we bring technical expertise and professional discipline into every aspect of our export operations.

Our background in horticulture allows us to understand crops beyond sourcing from varietal selection and maturity standards to post-harvest quality, shelf life, and export suitability. This knowledge-driven approach enables us to deliver consistent, export-grade products that meet international buyer expectations.

We work directly with farmers and handling partners to ensure that every product supplied is fresh, properly graded, and aligned with destination market requirements.

Our Mission

Our mission is to supply premium-quality coconuts and fresh produce to global markets by combining agricultural expertise, strict quality control, and customer-focused service. We are committed to:

  • Maintaining uncompromised quality at every stage
  • Ensuring reliable and timely deliveries
  • Understanding and meeting specific buyer requirements
  • Building long-term, trust-based relationships with our customers

Customer satisfaction is at the core of our operations, guiding our sourcing, handling, packaging, and export decisions.
